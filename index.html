SlicerITKUltrasoundÂ¶

A 3D Slicer [Fedorov2012] extension for ultrasound image formation, processing, and analysis. Interfaces built off the ITKUltrasound library [McCormick2014]. The modules available in this are useful for both 2D and 3D image generation for traditional B-mode imaging [McCormick2010], but also next-generation ultrasound image modalities like ultrasound spectroscopy [Aylward2016], acoustic radiation force imaging (ARFI) [Palmeri2016], acoustic radiation force shear wave imaging (ARFI-SWEI), and others.
